Story

A long time ago, climbing the Stetind mountain was considered foolhardy. Many tried, many failed. Today, with the help of skilled guides and modern equipment, even an inexperienced – but committed – mountaineer can reach the summit.

At Stetind Capital, we invest in brave, fun projects. We are an independent actor, and short-term gain is not our focus. We are patient. We look for sustainable projects that strengthen local communities and create jobs. Colourful projects that make people's lives better.

Kristoffer Lærdal founded Stetind Capital in 2018 to expand the scope of Tomte Forvaltning, which has successfully managed the Lærdal family’s financial portfolio since 2004.

Our history is part Norwegian, part Columbian, with Kristoffer originating from Córdoba, Quindío. Our heritage is the family values that have helped Laerdal Medical save lives for 75 years.

Our journey is just beginning.
